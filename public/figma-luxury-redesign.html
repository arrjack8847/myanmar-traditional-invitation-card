Mobile Priority Notes

Use 44px minimum tap targets for RSVP, audio, gallery, and map actions.

Keep names large and image-led; supporting copy stays short so mobile screens feel ceremonial, not crowded.

Luxury direction: antique gold accents, ivory paper, deep noir overlays, restrained wine and sage accents.

Cards stay crisp with 8px radius; the phone shell is the only highly rounded surface.
